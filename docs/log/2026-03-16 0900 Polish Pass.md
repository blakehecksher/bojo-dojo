## Full notes

### Renderer upgrades
- **60 FPS**: Removed 30fps throttle, render loop now runs at native refresh rate with dt cap at 50ms
- **Antialiasing**: Enabled `antialias: true` on WebGLRenderer
- **Tone mapping**: Switched from `NoToneMapping` to `ACESFilmicToneMapping` at 1.1 exposure for warmer, more cinematic look
- **Power preference**: Added `powerPreference: 'high-performance'` for mobile GPU selection

### Control feel
- **Camera momentum**: SwipeCamera now tracks velocity during drag and applies exponential decay (0.92 factor) after release — camera glides to a stop instead of stopping dead
- **Thumbstick snap-back**: Knob returns to center with a spring-like cubic-bezier overshoot animation on pointer release
- **Pull slider glow**: Handle gains a colored glow at high draw force for clearer force feedback

### Bow model
- **Idle sway**: Gentle figure-eight motion using dual sine waves (1.1 Hz X, 0.8 Hz Y) at ±3mm/±2mm amplitude
- **Fire recoil**: Quick upward kick (40mm up, 30mm back, -15° rotation) with cubic ease-out recovery over 350ms
- Both require new `update(dt)` method called every frame

### Crosshair
- **Draw feedback**: Crosshair shrinks from 24px to 14px as bow is drawn (tighter aim = tighter crosshair)
- **Glow**: Lines and center dot gain warm yellow-orange glow at high draw force
- **Dot growth**: Center dot grows from 4px to 6px during draw
- All transitions CSS-based (0.15s ease-out)

### Screen transitions
- **MenuScreen**: Fade-in/fade-out with 0.3s opacity transition. Button hover/active states with scale, background shift, and glow. Input focus states with gold border. Primary button (Create Game) has distinct gold styling
- **LobbyScreen**: Same fade transitions. Room code has warm text-shadow that flashes on copy. Share button changes to green "Copied!" state. Waiting text pulses. Player entries have subtle background
- **RoundEndScreen**: Title scales in from 0.5x with spring-like overshoot. Subtitle fades in with 0.2s delay. Both fade out with scale-down

### HUD animations
- **Arrow counter**: Pops to 1.3x scale on decrement with spring recovery. Color shifts to yellow at ≤2 arrows, red at 0
- **Timer**: Pulses at ≤10 seconds (scale 1→1.12 at 0.6s period). Gains red glow text-shadow. Turns red at ≤15s without pulse

### Player markers
- **Idle bob**: Gentle sine wave bob (1.5 Hz, ±50mm) with randomized phase per player
- **Hit flash**: Flashes white on hit via material color lerp over 300ms, then hides. Provides visual confirmation of hit before disappearing

### Arrow trails
- **Flight trail**: 12-segment line trail follows arrow during flight. Warm white color with fading alpha (1.0 → 0.0). Each frame shifts positions back and inserts new head position
- **Landing fade**: Trail fades to invisible over ~0.33s after arrow lands, then geometry is cleaned up

### Audio
- **Richer tones**: All placeholder sounds regenerated with harmonics, noise, and shaped envelopes instead of flat sine waves:
  - Bow draw: Rising pitch creak (80→200 Hz) with 3 harmonics + noise texture
  - Bow release: Sharp twang with rapid exponential decay and inharmonic overtone
  - Arrow land: Low thud (80/55 Hz) with noise burst impact
  - Arrow whiz: Dropping pitch whoosh with wind noise
  - Arrow flight: Subtle flutter with modulation
  - UI click: New — clean 800 Hz blip with rapid decay (60ms)

### HUD menu button
- Added hover/active states (background darken, slight scale)
